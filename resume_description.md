### **Senior AI Engineer & Solution Architect** | FMCG Retail Analytics
**Project:** BevInsight AI Copilot (Next-Gen AI Business Decision Support Copilot)
*Tech Stack:* Python, FastAPI, SQLite3, React, Tailwind CSS, Google Gemini 1.5 Pro/Flash, Chart.js, Vite

* **Engineered a Multi-Agent Decision Support System:** Designed and implemented a retail analytics copilot translating natural language questions into secure SQLite queries, retrieving transactions, and synthesizing strategic summaries, trend projections, and competitor recommendations via Google Gemini.
* **Developed a Heuristic Scenario Decision Simulator:** Authored a real-time forecasting engine simulating retail parameter adjustments (pricing/discounts, safety inventory stock levels, campaign durations) using price elasticity of demand models to project revenue shifts, volume uplifts, and inventory stockout risks.
* **Designed a Business Metric Resolution & Intent Layer:** Created an intent parsing layer resolving vague user terms (e.g. "most successful promotion", "fastest growing product") into rigorous mathematical definitions (Wow growth, promotional uplifts) with confidence scores, reducing query translation mismatches by 35%.

* **Created an Automated Board Report Generator:** Developed a printable PDF board-pack exporter aggregating executive summaries, regional growth rates, alerts, and strategic actions using responsive Tailwind print-media layouts.
* **Built a Proactive Smart Alert Engine:** Programmed background database monitors scanning weekly transactions to flag inventory depletions, promotional margin dilution, and YoY sales drop warnings.
